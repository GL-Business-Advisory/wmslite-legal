Account Deletion Request – WMSlite

This page describes how users of the WMSlite app (hereby referred to as "Application") created by GL Business Advisory Sdn. Bhd. (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") can request deletion of their account and associated data.


How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your WMSlite account and data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to contact.glba.dev@gmail.com with the subject line: Account Deletion Request – WMSlite
  2. Include the email address associated with your WMSlite account in the email body.
  3. The Service Provider will verify your identity and process your request within 14 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon a confirmed deletion request, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account credentials (email address and authentication records)
  • Your display name and user profile
  • All products and inventory records associated with your account
  • All transaction history associated with your account
  • All product images uploaded by you

What Data Is Retained

The following data may be retained after account deletion:

  • Purchase and subscription records — transaction records held by Google Play and RevenueCat may be retained in accordance with their respective data retention policies and applicable law.
  • Anonymised or aggregated data — any data that has been anonymised and cannot be linked back to you may be retained for service improvement purposes.

Retention Period

All personal data described under "What Data Is Deleted" above will be removed within 14 business days of your verified deletion request. Retained data such as purchase records may be held for up to 7 years in accordance with financial record-keeping obligations.


Contact Us

If you have any questions about this process, please contact the Service Provider via email at contact.glba.dev@gmail.com.
